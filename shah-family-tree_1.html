Maester's Notes
  • The lineage traces through Raichand → Jaychand → Hathidas → Kalidas (wed Maghu baa) before reaching the founders.
  • Kalidas had three sons: Natwarlal (father of Dilip, Annapurna & Sohag), Mangaldas (the patriarch), and Chandu (wed Jasi, departed without heirs).
  • Two women bear the name Kalavati — wed to Kantilal and Navneet (sons of Mangaldas) respectively.
  • Three women bear the name Kokila — wed to Madhusudan, Champaklal, and Ramanlal (1st wife) respectively.
  • Ramanlal first wed Kokila; Sameer and Rashmi were born of that union. He later wed Ranjan; Nilesh was born of that second union.
  • Urmila's lord-husband is Pravinchandra, known as PK.
  • Rasik Shah departed this world at ten years of age, leaving no heirs.
  • Sanjay, son of Madhusudan, perished in 1982 at twenty years of age, leaving no descendants.
  • Shruti, daughter of Shailesh, passed away in 2023. Her husband Tanay Madhiwala survives her.
  • Some branches of this lineage remain to be recorded.
